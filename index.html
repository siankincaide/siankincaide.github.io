About LSE Generate

Welcome to LSE Generate, the School’s home for entrepreneurship. Our programme supports students and alumni at any stage of building a socially responsible business, in the UK and worldwide.

Holborn, London

Funding Compeition

We run the LSE Generate funding competition twice a year, during the Michaelmas and Lent terms. Each competition has 3 to 5 winners, who will each take home a share of £20,000 to spend on their ventures

Generate Accelerator Programme

GAP is a 3-month part-time accelerator programme dedicated to the most promising early-stage startups co-founded by LSE students, staff, and alumni. We run one cohort a year with ten commercially viable startups that exhibit potential in attaining at least one of the UNSDGs through entrepreneurial activities.
